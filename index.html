Operation Doda – The Great Helmet Heist

Subject: Operation Doda – The Great Helmet Heist
Date: 11 November, Yammu Standard Time

Executive Summary

At approximately 2:42 PM, the Chairman’s Limited-Edition Polo Helmet — a sacred artifact containing encrypted coordinates, vintage malt aroma, and classified polo strategies — was reported missing from headquarters.

The Chairman himself remained composed and radiant, simply stating:
“Find it before sunset. I have martinis waiting on Mars.”

Panic ensued everywhere else.

Chronology of Events

1) Emergency Briefing (HQ – Hall of Confusion)

  • CEO Tak activated Code Duggar Red (“Everyone look serious”).
  • MD Verma nervously opened Excel.
  • Chairman Puri, calm as ever, sipped his single malt and signaled phase one: Divine Observation Mode.

2) Arrival of the Alien Asset

Pan Dea Chmyar — codename Khota X9 — descended from the rooftop antenna array, wearing sunglasses and a misplaced sense of confidence.

He announced: “Target located — Doda sector, near a bakery. Coordinates confirmed via pickle-jar triangulation.”

3) Field Deployment

The trio — Tak, Verma, and Chmyar — departed on CEO Tak’s vintage Lambretta scooter, registration YMU-420.
Verma demanded a “research detour” through Samba (sampled samosas for morale).
Chmyar requested permission to “slap for truth,” which was silently approved by a committee of one (himself).

The Chole Pathure Slap Incident (Doda Main Bazaar)

While searching for Bakery No. 3, the team stopped a man eating chole pathure. CEO Tak politely asked for directions. The man, still chewing, said, “Nahi pata bhaiya.”

Laffad #1 was deployed. Chmyar raised his mighty hand and delivered a precision strike across the man’s cheek: “How dare you not know your own city! The bakery is right behind you!”

The stunned man dropped his plate, turned around — and indeed, there it was. Trembling, he stammered, “Sorry sir… I am not from Yammu, I am from Bihar.”

At which point MD Verma executed Laffad #2 and thundered: “Phosdi de! You came to Yammu without a No-Objection Certificate from Chairman Sahab? Report to DuggarCorp Immigration immediately!”

Local witnesses clapped. The man apologized again and offered his remaining chole pathure as tribute.

The Doda Discovery

The bakery was raided. Helmet found beneath stale pastries, glowing faintly and playing Dogri folk remix in C minor.

Inside was a handwritten note: “Loyalty Test Complete. Congratulations, Barely. – Chairman Puri.”

Tak saluted, Verma posed for a selfie, and Chmyar saluted the helmet twice — once for Yammu, once for the Chairman’s glory.

The Duggar Tolo and Polo Club (DTPC)

All top DuggarCorp decisions are made inside the hallowed halls of the Duggar Tolo and Polo Club (DTPC) — a 100% subsidiary of DuggarCorp. It is the elite hangout where Yammu’s finest tolos and bandars come to learn polo, refine Duggar etiquette, and absorb top-tier strategy directly from Chairman Puri.

The club’s security is airtight, managed by Clerk Heera, the Junior Nightwatchman in Charge, commanding a battalion of 10,843 Bandars. Not even a mosquito enters without a pass counter-signed by the Chairman.

While DTPC carries a rich legacy of “Duggar Desh De Tolo Khelde Polo,” it also doubles as a luxurious resort for the Chairman’s personal pursuits. Chairman Puri often spends evenings scuba-diving, golfing, or sampling fine IPAs brewed in the club’s own brewery — a craft line so refined it’s exported under the label “Yammu Yeast Reserve.”

The club regularly hosts global icons and strategic partners: President Trump, Elon Musk, Satya Naddela, Judge Katil Banjara, Bob Cristo the Conman, and the top smuggler mitrons from France — all attending for the Chairman’s blessings and instruction in Duggar-style leadership, smuggling ethics, and interplanetary negotiation.
